Welcome to the source-backed website for **Local Generals.io (LocalGen)**, the open-source strategy game maintained by **SZXC Work Group**.

The upstream README keeps the promise simple: you can play generals.io-style matches **entirely offline**, rely on **ready-to-use built-in bots**, and even battle friends over the **same LAN**. This site turns that promise into a practical hub for release picking, bot study, simulator workflows, and bilingual contributor onboarding.

Two details matter immediately:

- **`master` / `v6.x`** is the active Qt-based rewrite and is still evolving.
- **GitHub Releases** remain the safest source for older stable builds and historical downloads.

Use this site when you want to decide which build to download, understand the built-in and external bot model, or contribute with the same terminology and expectations used in the project docs.
