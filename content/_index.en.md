Welcome to the official website for **Local Generals.io (LocalGen)**, an open-source project maintained by **SZXC Work Group**.

LocalGen lets you play a generals.io-inspired strategy game **entirely offline**, either against built-in bots or with friends over the same LAN. This website brings together project information, downloads, release history, contributor information, mirrored documentation, and bilingual onboarding material for both players and contributors.
